Shaquille O'Neal

American former professional basketball player who is a sports analyst on the television program Inside the NBA

Here is the timeline of Shaq's Basketball career:

  • 1972 - Born in Newwark, New Jersey,to Lucille O'Neal and Joe Toney.
  • 1989 - Commited to Louisiana State University for 4 years.
  • 1992 - Drafted into the NBA Round: 1 Pick: 1st by the Orlando Magic.
  • 1996 - Became a free agent and later joined the LA Lakers.
  • 2000 - Won his 1st NBA championship ring along with the NBA Finals MVP award.
  • 2001 - Won his 2nd NBA championship ring along with the NBA Finals MVP award.
  • 2002 - Won his 3rd NBA championship ring along with the NBA Finals MVP award.
  • 2003 - Suffered a toe injury and recovered over most of the season. The Lakers would fail to reach playoffs.
  • 2004 - Traded to the Miami Heat for Caron butler, Lamar Odom, Brian Grant, and future first-round draft choice.
  • 2006 - Won his 4th NBA championship ring
  • 2008 - Traded to the Phoenix Suns for Shawn Marion and Marcus Banks
  • 2009 - Traded to the Cleveland Cavaliers for Sasha Pavlovic, Ben Wallace, $500,000, and a 2010-second round draft pick.
  • 2010 - Aquired by the Boston Celtics.
  • 2011 - Announced his retirement via social media. On a short video on Twitter, O'Neal tweeted, "We did it. Nineteen years, baby. I want to thank you very much. That's why I'm telling you first. I'm about to retire. Love you. Talk to you soon."
